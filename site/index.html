Read before you trust a number here

  • Observational, not causal. n=969 MLPerf Training submissions (v3.1–v6.0). Submitters co-optimize topology with software and effort — the premium is an association, not a controlled experiment.
  • The comm-light curve is only marginally significant (p≈0.058); its 95% band crosses 1.0× at domain=72. Treat that curve as directional, not precisely sized.
  • Market prices are list prices, not verified transactions — on-demand rates from 3 cloud providers, n=13 raw (n=8 after removing GPUs with no measured MLPerf generation). Even after generation-adjustment, domain size and GPU generation remain confounded in this snapshot; it is a descriptive comparison, not a matched estimate.
  • Findings have not been reviewed or verified by MLCommons.
